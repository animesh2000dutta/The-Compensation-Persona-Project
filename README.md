In this project, I transformed a dataset of 400+ employee records from a Canadian startup into a strategic Salary Intelligence Dashboard designed to inform B2B marketing targeting and sales compensation planning. 
By analysing compensation patterns across 11 job roles, I uncovered that IT Managers ($170K+), Cloud Solutions Architects ($160K+), and DevOps Engineers ($161K+) represent the highest-value B2B personas — ideal targets for account-based marketing campaigns and sales outreach. 
The analysis also revealed a compelling 70% salary growth trajectory from entry-level to senior roles, validating career development as a powerful messaging angle for upscaling and recruitment marketing. 
Perhaps most notably, I discovered a 20% gender pay gap favouring women in IT Security Analyst roles, alongside a 7% gap favouring men in IT Management — insights that inform both DEI-focused marketing strategies and equitable compensation planning. 
The final interactive Excel dashboard features dynamic slicers, KPI cards, and role-based benchmarking, enabling stakeholders to segment audiences, benchmark compensation, and derive actionable go-to-market intelligence in real time.
